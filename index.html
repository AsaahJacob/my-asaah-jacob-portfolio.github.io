About Me

Jacob Asaah is an electrical engineering graduate with a passion for STEM education and innovation. With extensive experience in tutoring and mentoring, he has honed his skills in circuit design, robotics, programming, and troubleshooting. Jacob's commitment to education extends beyond the classroom, as evidenced by his participation in workshops and conferences. His internship at Katharos Limited further enriched his practical knowledge in electrical engineering. Jacob holds a Higher National Diploma in Electrical Engineering from Accra Technical University and an International Certificate in Montessori Theory and Methodology from United Montessori College. Proficient in a wide range of technical skills, Jacob excels in teamwork and technical report writing. He aspires to contribute to the advancement of STEM education and innovation.
